Admission Procedure for Pre-CAT Course and Modular Course

This process is for part time courses offered at SunBeam Pune.

Currently we offer Pre-C-DAC courses which prepares students for section A,B & C of C-CAT.

Before registration, student is requested to search all information about C-DAC PG courses, course contents, Sunbeam's Pre-CAT Batch timings etc.

After finalization of batch, student should register on this site using Admission Registration link.

After successful registration student will receive an email regarding registration no, payment, etc. Please follow the link for payment gateway to pay by Debit / Credit Card ONLY. (Please DO NOT USE any other payment format)

Alternatively use bank challan attached in the email. It is printed as per your name, course and batch ID. Please visit nearest ICICI bank branch & pay the fees as per challan . Bank will accept the fees only in Cash.

Student will receive payment recipt and the list of nearby private hostels in email once the payment is done within 3 working days.

Kindly carry hard copy of your payment proof on the first day of the course and submit it at the admission team.
