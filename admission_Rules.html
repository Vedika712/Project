RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Registration Forms are available only at http://admission.sunbeaminfo.com/aspx/CourseSchedule.aspx
  • Students have to fill the Online Registration Form for admission. There is no any other method for registration for part time courses.
  • Before registration please go through the Course Contents, Batch Schedule, Timings, Fees detail and Eligibility Criteria.
  • Students are not required to submit print out of Registration Form and any documents.
  • Course Fees once paid is non refundable and non transferable.
  • Admission is confirmed only after receipt of payment at bank on or before last date of payment as per course schedule.
  • Kindly follow the instructions on "Procedure" page to pay the fees.
  • Course Caution Deposit (refundable) can be deposited through Online payment / Internet Banking. The Course Caution deposit will be refunded at the end of course in online mode after deducting some amount against extra educational services that will be provided during the six months of the course and campus placement period in case of any.
  • Please carry the Institute Copy of fees paid receipt on the first day of the batch and submit the same in the office.
  • Identity Cards will be issued on first day of the batch.
    • Identity Card is must while entering in the SunBeam Campus. Students without Identity Card are not allowed to attend the Lecture or Lab.
  • If you lose your Identity Card a duplicate Identity Card will be issued at an additional cost.
  • Make sure that you are present in Lecture Hall and Lab at least 5 min before the scheduled time
  • Student will be responsible for the loss of Lecture or Lab sessions due to their leave / absenteeism.
  • Please switch off your Mobile Phones whenever you are in lecture hall or lab.
Course Fees to be paid on or before last date of payment.
